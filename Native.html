Gray Catbird
The Gray Catbird is a member of the family Mimidae, and can be commonly mistaken for the Dark-Eyed Junco, another gray bird found in the city. However, the Gray Catbird is distinctly larger and has a dark beak and legs, with a black cap. It received its name due to its distinctly harsh call, which sounds like the meow of a cat. The Mimidae family also comprises of the Mockingbird and Thrashers, known for their mimicking abilities of other birds and even animals.
